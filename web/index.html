Import your Infinite Craft data

Importing your game data allows The Wiki to show you which elements you have already crafted and simplify crafting recipes using your existing elements. It will NOT show any new discoveries, or elements the wiki doesn't know about yet.

Step 1 - Click and drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar

Sync with Wiki

Step 2 - Navigate to Infinite Craft, and click on the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.

Step 3 - From your open session of Infinite Craft, the script in the bookmarklet will open a popup to the Wiki, which will sync any changes you make in your game. (Make sure popups are allowed!)
